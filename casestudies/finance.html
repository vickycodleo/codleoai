AI Powered Digital Agents for Smarter, Faster, and Compliant Financial Experiences

Financial services and FinTech organizations operate in a highly regulated, trustdriven environment where speed, accuracy, and compliance are critical. Customers expect instant access to loan details, repayments, credit eligibility, and support across digital channels while institutions must ensure data security, regulatory compliance, and operational efficiency. At the same time, FinTech companies manage complex financial products such as loans, credit lines, repayments, foreclosures, and KYC workflows, often across multiple systems. Manual processes, fragmented data, and high customer inquiry volumes can slow response times and increase operational costs.

How Agentforce Helps Financial Institutions

The Challenge

Agentforce is a purposebuilt AI agent layer on Salesforce that enables banks, NBFCs, and FinTech companies to deliver secure, compliant, and highly personalized experiences across the customer lifecycle. By working natively with Salesforce data, workflows, and permission models, Agentforce ensures every interaction is contextual, accurate, auditable, and aligned with regulatory and business rules.

Agentforce enables FinTech and Financial Institutions to:

Automate high-volume financial service interactions intelligently

Provide customers with real-time access to loan and account information

Assist internal teams with insights, recommendations, and next-best actions

Maintain trust, compliance, and data security at scale

AI Customer Service Agent (Loan & Account Servicing)

Handles customer queries related to loans, repayments, EMIs, foreclosure, statements, and account information across chat, email, WhatsApp, and voice channels.

What the Agent Can Do

  • Fetch real-time loan details including amount, tenure, interest rate, and outstanding balance
  • Share EMI schedules, repayment history, and due dates
  • Generate and share secure repayment links
  • Provide foreclosure and part-payment details with charges and eligibility
  • Answer FAQs related to loan terms, penalties, and policies
  • Escalate complex cases to human agents with full context

Business Impact

  • Reduced call center and customer support costs
  • Faster query resolution and improved turnaround time (TAT)
  • Higher customer satisfaction (CSAT) and trust

Loan Origination & Eligibility Agent

Supports customers and sales teams during loan application, credit assessment, and onboarding.

What the Agent Can Do

  • Perform real-time eligibility checks based on customer profile and business rules
  • Integrate with credit bureaus for credit score and risk assessment
  • Guide customers step-by-step through the loan application process
  • Validate KYC documents and track onboarding status

Answers Questions Like

  • Am I eligible for this loan?
  • What documents are required?
  • What is my credit limit?

Business Impact

  • Faster loan approvals and streamlined onboarding
  • Reduced drop-offs during application journeys
  • Improved conversion rates and revenue growth

Repayment, Collections & Recovery Agent

Assists in proactive repayment reminders, collections, and recovery workflows while maintaining customer-friendly engagement.

What the Agent Can Do

  • Send automated repayment reminders and multi-channel notifications
  • Share secure payment and repayment links
  • Answer queries related to overdue amounts, penalties, and dues
  • Suggest restructuring, extension, or settlement options based on rules
  • Assist internal collections teams with customer insights and follow-up prioritization

Business Impact

  • Improved on-time repayments
  • Lower delinquency and NPA rates
  • Reduced manual follow-ups by collections teams

Sales & Relationship Management Agent

Empowers relationship managers and sales teams with actionable insights to grow wallet share and retain customers.

What the Agent Can Do

  • Summarize customer financial relationships and complete loan history
  • Identify upsell and cross-sell opportunities such as top-up loans, credit cards, and insurance
  • Recommend next-best actions based on customer behavior and lifecycle stage

Answers Questions Like

  • Which customers are eligible for a top-up loan?
  • Which accounts need follow-up today?

Business Impact

  • Increased sales productivity
  • Higher cross-sell and upsell conversion rates
  • Improved customer lifetime value (CLV)

Operations & Process Automation Agent

Automates internal financial operations across underwriting, risk, operations, and support teams.

What the Agent Can Do

  • Handle approvals, data updates, and real-time status checks
  • Automate internal service requests and cross-team workflows
  • Guide teams through standardized financial and operational processes
  • Reduce dependency on manual handoffs between systems

Business Impact

  • Faster operational turnaround times
  • Reduced errors, rework, and operational risk
  • Improved employee efficiency and experience

Compliance, Risk & Audit Agent

Ensures regulatory compliance, audit readiness, and risk mitigation across financial operations.

What the Agent Can Do

  • Monitor KYC, AML, and internal policy adherence in real time
  • Flag exceptions, risks, and potential compliance violations proactively
  • Provide compliant, guided responses aligned with RBI and internal policies
  • Maintain audit trails and enable explainable, regulator-ready decision support

Business Impact

  • Reduced regulatory and compliance risk
  • Improved audit readiness, transparency, and traceability
  • Consistent, compliant, and defensible decision-making
